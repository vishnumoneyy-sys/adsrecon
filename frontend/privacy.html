Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ADSRECON ("we," "us," or "our") is a Meta Ads Library intelligence tool. We collect the following information:

  • Facebook Ad Data: We access publicly available Meta Ad Library data through the official Facebook Graph API, including ad creative text, advertiser names, delivery dates, and spending estimates. This data is publicly available on Meta's Ad Library.
  • User Preferences: Search queries and filter settings you apply within the tool. These are stored locally in your browser.
  • API Access Token: Your Facebook Graph API access token is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display Meta Ad Library data for competitor research and market analysis
  • To classify ads by category (nutra verticals, health supplements, etc.)
  • To track and save ads you mark as favorites
  • To improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage

ADSRECON stores data locally on your device in a SQLite database. No data is uploaded to external servers. Your Facebook API token is stored locally in a .fb_token file on your machine.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. All data accessed through the Meta Ads Library API is publicly available information.

5. Meta / Facebook Data

ADSRECON accesses Meta's official Ad Library API. Any data retrieved is subject to Meta's Data Policy and Ad Library API Terms. We encourage users to review Meta's policies.

6. Cookies

ADSRECON does not use cookies. All preferences are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Your API token is stored locally on your device — protect it as you would any sensitive credential.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
