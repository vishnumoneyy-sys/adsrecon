ADSRECON stores data locally in your browser only. We do not maintain a server-side database of your personal information. This page explains how you can delete all data associated with your use of the ADSRECON browser extension and service.

Option 1: Delete All Local Data (Immediate)

Since ADSRECON stores data locally in your browser, deletion is immediate and does not require contacting us:

  1. Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions
  2. Find ADSRECON in your installed extensions list
  3. Click Details to expand extension info
  4. Click Storage (or "Extension storage")
  5. Click Clear all data to remove all stored ads, tracked pages, saved searches, API tokens, and settings

Alternatively: Right-click the ADSRECON icon in your browser toolbar → Options → Clear All Data button.

What Data Gets Deleted

Clearing ADSRECON extension storage removes:

  • All saved ads and tracked pages
  • Filter settings and search preferences
  • Facebook/Meta API access token (if configured)
  • Extension state, history, and cache
  • CSV exports and downloaded data

Option 2: Request Server-Side Data Deletion

If you have contacted us and we hold any of your personal data on our servers, we will delete it within 30 days of receiving your verified request, in accordance with applicable data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA.

Data We Receive from Meta

ADSRECON accesses publicly available advertising data from Meta's Ad Library API. This data is collected directly by you through the extension and is not stored on our servers. Meta's own data policies govern their Ad Library data. To understand how Meta handles your data, review Meta's Privacy Policy.

Note: Deleting data from ADSRECON does not affect data held by Meta Platforms, Inc. Contact Meta directly for their data retention and deletion policies regarding your activity on their platforms.
